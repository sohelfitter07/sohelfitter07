⭐ Weekly Rules

  • Junk food (chips, cookies, Doritos) → only 2–3 days per week.
  • Screen time – weekdays: 30 min max; weekends: 1 hour after chores.
  • Outdoor activity – every day, at least 1 hour.
  • Wake up & bedtime – even on weekends, within 30 minutes of these times.
  • “First work, then play.” No extra screen until homework is done.
