🍎 Ayaan’s Weekly Meal Plan (for Mom)

Balanced meals for energy, focus, and growth. Junk food limited to 2–3 days/week.

🥣 Breakfast (7:45–8:15 AM) – Protein + healthy carbs + fruit
DayIdea
MonScrambled eggs + whole wheat toast + sliced banana
TueOatmeal with milk + chopped dates/walnuts + apple slices
WedGreek yogurt + berries + boiled egg
ThuPeanut butter sandwich on multigrain bread + small glass of milk
FriWhole grain pancakes (2 small) + 1 fried egg + orange wedges
SatCheese omelette + half a pear
SunSmoothie bowl (yogurt, spinach, mango, flaxseed) topped with granola
🥪 Packed Lunch (school, 12:00-12:30 PM) – Protein + veg/fruit + whole grain
DayIdea
MonTurkey/cheese roll-ups (2) + carrot sticks + hummus + grapes + water
TueLeftover chicken + rice + cucumber slices + orange
WedWhole wheat pasta with tomato sauce + cherry tomatoes + yogurt tube
ThuEgg salad sandwich (whole wheat) + apple wedges + cheese stick
FriTuna or chickpea salad + whole grain crackers + bell pepper strips + small banana
Sat (if school)Quinoa + black beans + corn + avocado pieces + berries
SunLeftovers or homemade pizza slice + salad
🍪 After‑School Snack (4:15–4:30 PM) – Pick one, protein + fiber
  • Apple slices + peanut butter
  • Banana + small yogurt
  • Cheese stick + whole grain crackers
  • Handful of almonds + raisins
  • Homemade smoothie (milk/yogurt + berries + honey)
  • Hard‑boiled egg + a few crackers

Junk food rule: Cookies, chips, Doritos only 2–3 times/week (e.g., Tuesday & Friday). Serve a small bowl.

💡 Quick tips for mom:
• Portion size for 8‑year‑old: about ½ to ⅔ of adult portion. Let Ayaan ask for more.
• Drinks: water, milk, buttermilk, homemade lemonade. Avoid soda & packaged juice.
• Prep on Sunday: boil eggs, cut veggies, cook extra rice/quinoa.
• If Ayaan refuses new food – offer again another day without pressure (may take 10–15 tries).
• Involve him in choosing between two options: “broccoli or cucumber?”
