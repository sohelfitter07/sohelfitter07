Common Rower Problems We Fix

Inconsistent Resistance

If your rower's resistance feels uneven or fluctuates unexpectedly, it could indicate issues with the resistance mechanism or calibration.

We diagnose and recalibrate resistance systems with a 90-day warranty.

Grinding Noises

Unusual sounds during rowing often indicate worn bearings, chain/belt issues, or problems with the flywheel assembly.

We identify and fix noise sources within 24 hours.

Monitor Failure

From blank screens to inaccurate metrics, we troubleshoot and repair all monitor issues across all major brands.

Specialized repair for rowing machine monitors with same-day service.

Chain/Belt Issues

If your chain/belt slips, sticks, or feels loose, it may need adjustment or replacement for smooth operation.

Precise chain/belt tensioning and replacement services.

Seat Problems

Seats that stick, wobble, or come off track prevent proper rowing form and can indicate rail issues.

We repair seat mechanisms and realign rails with OEM parts.

Footplate Detachment

Loose or detached footplates affect safety and performance during rowing sessions.

Professional reinstallation and reinforcement services.
