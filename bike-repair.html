Common Exercise Bike Problems We Fix

Resistance Failure

If your bike's resistance isn't changing, it could be due to a faulty controller, magnetic system failure, or worn brake pads.

We diagnose and repair all resistance issues with a 90-day warranty.

Grinding Noises

Grinding or squeaking sounds often indicate worn bearings, misaligned belts, or damaged flywheel components.

We identify and fix the source of the noise within 24 hours.

Console Errors

From blank screens to connectivity issues, we troubleshoot and repair all console and display problems.

Specialized repair for all major brands with same-day service.

Pedal Issues

If your pedals are slipping, sticking, or making noise, it may need crank replacement or bearing service.

Professional pedal and crank service to restore smooth operation.

Power Problems

Inconsistent power or failure to start can indicate electrical issues, damaged cords, or console malfunctions.

We repair electrical systems with OEM parts and safety testing.

Seat Adjustments

If your seat won't stay in position or moves unexpectedly, it may need replacement parts or adjustment mechanisms.

Comprehensive seat and adjustment system repairs.
