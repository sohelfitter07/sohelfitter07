Our Journey Since 2013

Founded in 2013, Canadian Fitness Repair was born out of a passion for fitness and a commitment to excellence. Our founder, a certified technician with over 15 years of experience in the fitness industry, recognized a gap in the market for reliable, expert repair services for both commercial and residential fitness equipment.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into a team of highly skilled technicians serving the Greater Toronto Area and beyond. We've expanded our services while maintaining our core values of integrity, expertise, and customer-first service.

Today, we're proud to be GTA's premier fitness equipment repair service, trusted by gym owners, fitness centers, and home users alike. Our journey continues as we innovate and expand our services to meet the evolving needs of the fitness community.

Why Choose Us
