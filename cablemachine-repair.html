Common Strength Equipment Problems

Cable Fraying or Snapping

Frayed or broken cables compromise safety and functionality of your strength machines.

We replace cables with commercial-grade steel cables and properly tension them.

Stuck Adjustment Mechanisms

Difficulty adjusting seat heights or weight settings indicates internal issues.

We repair or replace adjustment knobs, pins, and internal mechanisms.

Torn Upholstery

Ripped or worn padding creates discomfort and hygiene issues.

We replace padding with commercial-grade vinyl that withstands heavy use.

Noisy Operation

Squeaking, grinding or rattling noises during use often indicate wear.

We identify and fix the source - usually bearings, bushings or loose parts.

Uneven Resistance

Inconsistent resistance during movement indicates cable or pulley issues.

We recalibrate tension systems and replace worn components.

Weight Stack Issues

Sticking weight plates or selector pin problems affect performance.

We align weight stacks, lubricate guide rods, and replace selector pins.
