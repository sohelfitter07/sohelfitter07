Common Walking Pad Problems We Fix

Walking Pad Won't Start

If your walking pad isn't powering on, it could be due to a blown fuse, faulty power switch, or damaged power cord.

We diagnose and repair all power issues with a 90-day warranty.

Strange Noises

Squeaking, grinding or knocking sounds often indicate worn bearings, loose components, or motor issues.

We identify and fix the source of the noise within 24 hours.

Remote Connectivity Issues

From unresponsive remotes to Bluetooth pairing problems, we troubleshoot and repair all control issues.

Specialized repair for all major brands with same-day service.

Speed Inconsistency

If your walking pad speed fluctuates unexpectedly, it may need drive belt, motor controller, or sensor replacement.

Precision calibration to restore smooth operation.

Folding Mechanism Failure

Folding mechanism failures prevent proper storage and can indicate serious structural issues.

We repair locking mechanisms, hinges, and supports with OEM parts.

Belt Issues

From slipping belts to tracking problems, these issues affect performance and safety.

Professional belt alignment, tensioning, and replacement services.
