How Our Repair Process Works

At Canadian Fitness Repair, we've streamlined our repair process to ensure maximum convenience and efficiency for our customers. Here's what you can expect when you choose our expert service:

1

Schedule Your Service

Contact us by phone, email, or through our online form to schedule a convenient appointment time. We offer same-day and next-day appointments for urgent repairs.

2

On-Site Diagnosis

Our certified technician arrives at your location at the scheduled time. We'll perform a comprehensive diagnostic assessment to identify all issues with your equipment.

3

Transparent Quote

After diagnosis, we'll provide you with a detailed, transparent quote for parts and labor. No hidden fees or surprises - you approve all work before we proceed.

4

Expert Repair

Using genuine parts and manufacturer-approved techniques, our technician will repair your equipment while you watch. Most repairs are completed in a single visit.

5

Quality Testing

We thoroughly test all repaired components to ensure they meet our high standards. You'll have a chance to test the equipment yourself before we leave.

6

Warranty & Support

All repairs come with our satisfaction guarantee and 90-day warranty. We provide after-service support to answer any questions you might have.
