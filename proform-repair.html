Common ProForm Problems We Fix

Treadmill Issues

  • Belt slipping or not moving
  • Incline/decline not working (Pro 2000 models)
  • Console display problems
  • Motor overheating or failure
  • Error codes (E1, E2, E3)
  • Walking belt alignment issues

Elliptical & Bike Problems

  • Stride mechanism malfunctions
  • Resistance not changing (iFit compatibility issues)
  • Squeaking or grinding noises
  • iFit connectivity issues
  • Power supply failures
  • Pedal and crank assembly problems

Smart Features & Technology

  • Touchscreen console repairs
  • iFit module replacement
  • Bluetooth connectivity issues
  • Software/firmware updates
  • Heart rate monitor calibration
  • Preventative maintenance programs
