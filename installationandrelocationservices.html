Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our installation services

How long does installation typically take?

Most single equipment installations take 1-2 hours. Complex setups or multiple pieces may take 2-4 hours. We'll provide a time estimate when you book your service.

Do I need to be present during the installation?

Yes, we recommend that you or an authorized adult be present during the installation to approve the placement and receive operational instructions.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Hamilton and the Greater Toronto Area including Burlington, Oakville, Mississauga, Ancaster, Dundas, Stoney Creek, and surrounding communities.

Do you offer same-day installation?

Yes, we offer same-day installation when scheduling permits. Please call us at (289) 925-7239 to check same-day availability.

What if my equipment is still in the box?

No problem! We're experts at unboxing and assembling equipment directly from manufacturers. We'll handle everything from unboxing to final setup.

Do you provide parts if something is missing or broken?

We carry common replacement parts for most major brands. If specialized parts are needed, we'll help you contact the manufacturer and schedule a return visit once parts arrive.
