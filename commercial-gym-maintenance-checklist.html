Daily Maintenance Tasks

Treadmills

  • Inspect safety clips and emergency stop
  • Wipe down console, handrails, and surfaces
  • Check belt alignment and tension
  • Listen for unusual noises during operation

Ellipticals

  • Clean stride rails and wheels
  • Check pedal tightness and alignment
  • Test all resistance levels
  • Verify console functions properly

Exercise Bikes

  • Inspect pedals and crank arms
  • Check seat adjustment mechanism
  • Test resistance system
  • Wipe down handles and seat

Strength Equipment

  • Inspect cables for fraying or damage
  • Check pulley alignment and function
  • Test all adjustment mechanisms
  • Wipe down upholstery and handles

Weekly Maintenance Tasks

Cardio Equipment

  • Lubricate treadmill belts (if required)
  • Vacuum motor vents and air intakes
  • Check drive belt tension
  • Inspect power cords and connections

Strength Equipment

  • Lubricate guide rods on weight stacks
  • Check pin and selector mechanisms
  • Inspect cable ends and attachments
  • Tighten all bolts and fasteners

General Facility

  • Test emergency stop functions
  • Check first aid kits and AED
  • Inspect flooring for damage
  • Verify all safety signs are visible

Monthly Maintenance Tasks

Preventative Maintenance

  • Calibrate electronic consoles
  • Check and adjust belt alignment
  • Inspect drive belts and motors
  • Test incline/decline mechanisms

Deep Cleaning

  • Deep clean upholstery
  • Clean under and around equipment
  • Sanitize high-touch surfaces
  • Clean air vents and cooling fans

Hamilton Seasonal Considerations

Winter Maintenance

  • Increase lubrication frequency
  • Monitor humidity levels
  • Check for condensation on electronics
  • Ensure proper heating in equipment areas

Summer Maintenance

  • Ensure proper ventilation
  • Monitor equipment temperatures
  • Increase cleaning frequency
  • Check air conditioning systems
