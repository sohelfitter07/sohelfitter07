Common Treadmill Problems We Fix

Treadmill Won't Start

If your treadmill isn't powering on, it could be due to a blown fuse, faulty power switch, or damaged power cord.

We diagnose and repair all power issues with a 90-day warranty.

Strange Noises

Squeaking, grinding or knocking sounds often indicate worn bearings, loose components, or motor issues.

We identify and fix the source of the noise within 24 hours.

Console Errors

From blank screens to error codes, we troubleshoot and repair all console and display issues.

Specialized repair for all major brands with same-day service.

Speed Inconsistency

If your treadmill speed fluctuates unexpectedly, it may need drive belt, motor controller, or sensor replacement.

Precision calibration to restore smooth operation.

Incline Failure

Incline mechanism failures prevent proper workout customization and can indicate serious issues.

We repair motors, controllers, and sensors with OEM parts.

Belt Issues

From slipping belts to tracking problems, these issues affect performance and safety.

Professional belt alignment, tensioning, and replacement services.
