Common Precor Problems We Fix

Treadmill Issues

  • Belt slipping or not moving
  • Incline motor failure (9 Series)
  • Console display problems
  • Motor controller failures
  • Error codes (E1, E2, E4)
  • Walking belt alignment issues

Elliptical & AMT Problems

  • Stride mechanism malfunctions
  • Resistance not changing (Precor AMT)
  • Squeaking or grinding noises
  • Touchscreen connectivity issues
  • Power supply failures
  • Drive belt replacement

Commercial Gym Equipment

  • Precor commercial series maintenance
  • Touchscreen console repairs
  • Drive system calibration
  • Deck replacement and lubrication
  • Electronic sensor calibration
  • Preventative maintenance programs
